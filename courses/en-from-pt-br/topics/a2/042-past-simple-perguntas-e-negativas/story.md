# História: Past simple com perguntas e negativas

Nível: A2

## História em inglês

### The missing update

On Thursday morning, the support team received several reports about an old dashboard. The dashboard didn't show the latest sales data. Nina asked, "Did the nightly import finish?" Her colleague answered, "No, it didn't. The final job didn't run." Nina then asked, "When did the previous step finish?" The logs showed that it finished at 2:14 a.m.

### A simple investigation

The team checked the scheduler, but they didn't find a configuration error. Nina asked another question: "Did anyone restart the worker yesterday?" One engineer said, "Yes, I did, but I didn't restart the scheduler." They discovered that the worker started before the scheduler was ready. The team restarted both services and ran the import again. This time, the job finished and the dashboard displayed the correct data.

## Notas de vocabulário

- didn't show = não mostrou; negativa no past simple
- Did the nightly import finish? = pergunta sobre uma ação concluída
- didn't run = não executou; run fica na forma base
- When did the previous step finish? = pergunta pelo momento em que algo aconteceu
- Did anyone restart the worker? = pergunta para identificar uma ação passada
- Yes, I did. = resposta curta afirmativa

## Perguntas de compreensão

1. What problem did the dashboard have?
2. Did the nightly import finish?
3. When did the previous step finish?
4. Did the team find a configuration error?
5. Who restarted the worker?
6. What didn't the engineer restart?

## Prática com a história

1. Transforme em pergunta: The worker started before the scheduler.
2. Transforme em negativa: They found a configuration error.
3. Responda afirmativamente: Did the final import finish?
4. Faça uma pergunta com why sobre a falha da tarefa.
